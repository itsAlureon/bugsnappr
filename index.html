Privacy Policy for Bugsnappr

Last updated:09/16/2025

Data Collection

Bugsnappr collects the following data only when you explicitly use the extension:

  • Website Content: Screenshots and GIF recordings of webpages when you click capture buttons
  • Page Information: URL and page title when you enable "Include metadata" option

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Data Usage

Collected data is used solely for:

  • Displaying captured screenshots and GIFs in the extension
  • Adding optional metadata overlays for bug reporting context
  • Enabling editing and annotation features

Data Deletion

You can delete any saved screenshots or GIFs at any time through the extension's "Saved Content" section. Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. All data remains on your local device.
